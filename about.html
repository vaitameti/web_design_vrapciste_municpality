History

200 BC

During 200 BC Vrapciste was under the control of Macedonian Kingdom and was mainly spoken todays Greek.

700-1500

During the period of 700-1500 the Slavs has came to the balkans and the people of vrapciste has adopted the culture of slavs

1500-1900

For almost 400 years the ottomans has been ruler of the balkans and the people of vrapciste during these period has adopted turkish as their mother tounge.

Information

Todays figures

Vrapciste today is the most populous suburb in Macedonia and consists of three ethnicities which all live in peace. There are around ten thousand people living in the village.Thanks to Vrapciste municpality we have have been able to provide 10 thousand people their needs in transportation and basic utilities.
